Let's play a game with dragons.

INSTRUCTIONS

Picking up the sword makes the dragon angry.

Each time you swing the sword, you have an equally random chance of hitting or missing the dragon. If you miss the dragon, he wins.

If you hit the dragon, you inflict damage equal to one to five points. Four points' worth of damage kills the dragon.

You are wandering through the forest, minding your own business, when you reach a meadow. You notice a sword and a few pieces of debris that might once have been armor near the center of the clearing.

Suddenly, a dragon swoops through the air, hovering menacingly over the meadow. What do you do?
